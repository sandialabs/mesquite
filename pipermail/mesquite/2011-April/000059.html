The triangles on the mesh boundary are the problem.  Mesquite
will evaluate the quality metric for all input elements.  The
inverse mean ratio metric cannot be evaluated for triangles (or
quadrilateral elements) without knowing the normal of the geometric surface
they mesh (the domain).
