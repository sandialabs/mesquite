Iulian,

  Here's what you need to do.  In mesquite/src/Misc/MsqDebug.hpp, at the top right before the #defines, add the line "#define  MSQ_ENABLE_DEBUG 1,2,3".  This will enable the maximum level of debug trace output.  Read the comments in MsqDebug.hpp for additional information of how debug tracing works in Mesquite.

If you have any problems or additional questions, be sure to let us know.

Good Luck,
